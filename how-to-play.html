How to play memoRise

  • We'll show you a series of random images one at a time.
  • If you spot a repeated image, hit the spacebar to mark it.
  • If an image is not a repeat, simply wait for the next one.
  • At the end, we'll display your score, reavealing how accurate your memory is.
